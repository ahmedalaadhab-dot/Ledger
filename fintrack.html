or tap to browse — CSV, XLS/XLSX or PDF

FinTrack reads each file on this device and saves everything to a local database in your browser — nothing is uploaded anywhere. CSV and Excel statements parse most reliably. PDF statements are read with best-effort line detection, since every bank formats them differently — always check the review screen before confirming. Transfers (Fawri+, BenefitPay, wire) aren't auto-tagged since they're used for everything — the first transfer to an account asks for a category, then FinTrack remembers it for that account. Anything else it can't confidently tag is left Uncategorized and you'll be asked too.
